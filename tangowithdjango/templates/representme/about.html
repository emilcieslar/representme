    Project

    Does your local MSP represent you? Sign up to RepresentME to decide for yourself.

    Your local MSP is elected by YOU to represent your constituency. They vote on which laws should be passed through the Scottish Parliament.

    By using RepresentME, you can check how your local MSP has voted on the laws that matter most to you. You can then vote on whether you feel a given law should have been passed or not. By sharing how you would have voted, you will build a measure of how closely your opinions match those of your MSP. You can check your percentage of similarity (0% as no agreement and 100% as complete agreement) with each of the local MSPs in your constituency.

    You can also engage with your MSP by commenting on each law. Join the discussion and progress from ‘Noob’ to ‘Newbie’ to ‘Master’ as you become more involved.

    Also, if you are a local MSP, sign up to engage with your constituents and discuss their views.
